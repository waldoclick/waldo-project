<objective>
Fix the cross-ad dedup bug in `recordView` so the views counter on `/anuncios/[slug]` increments per ad.

Root cause (already verified — do NOT re-investigate): `recordView` computes
`visitor_hash = sha256(ip|ua|day)` WITHOUT the ad, and the dedup `findMany` filters
only by `visitor_hash` + `viewed_at`, not by ad. So once a visitor views ANY ad in a
day, every other ad's view is deduped away and its counter freezes (e.g. ad 70 stuck at 0).

Fix: make dedup per visitor + ad + day by (a) including `adDocumentId` in the hash and
(b) adding `ad: ad.id` to the dedup `findMany` where clause as a belt-and-suspenders guard.

Purpose: Correct per-ad view tracking; the counter on the public ad detail page must increment.
Output: Patched service + updated unit tests + recorded live end-to-end verification.
</objective>
