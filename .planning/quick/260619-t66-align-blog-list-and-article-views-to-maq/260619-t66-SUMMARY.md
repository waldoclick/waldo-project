## Key fix (root-caused, not guessed)
The featured-ads fetch initially 500'd with "obj.hasOwnProperty is not a function".
Root cause: `useApiClient()` must be created at setup top-level — calling it after
the `await`s inside the useAsyncData handler reads the Nuxt request context outside
a valid instance (documented in useApiClient.ts itself). Fixed by creating the
client at top-level and reducing featured ads to a flat payload-safe `FeaturedAd`
shape (primitives only) to also keep devalue happy.
