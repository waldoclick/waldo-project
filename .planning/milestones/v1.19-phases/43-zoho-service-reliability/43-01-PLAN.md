1. `Zoho-oauthtoken` header prefix (replaces `Bearer`)
2. 401 response interceptor that clears `accessToken`, calls `refreshAccessToken()`, and retries
3. Optional `adapter` constructor param for `axios-mock-adapter` injection in tests
</objective>
