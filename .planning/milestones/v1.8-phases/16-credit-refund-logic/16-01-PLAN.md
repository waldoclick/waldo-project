<objective>
Wire reservation-freeing (credit refund) logic into `rejectAd()` and `bannedAd()` in the Strapi ad service. When either method runs, any linked `ad_reservation` or `ad_featured_reservation` is set to `ad = null` — making the reservation immediately reusable. This is identical to the pattern already used in `user.cron.ts` (lines 71–77) for expired free ads.

Purpose: Ensure users don't permanently lose reservation credits when their ad is rejected or banned by an admin.
Output: Modified `ad.ts` with refund logic in both methods; reservations are freed before the email is sent (so Phase 17 can reference the freed state in email templates).
</objective>
